Here are some things to know about computers in education:
Educational technology
The study of how to design, develop, and evaluate the learning process, instructional environment, and learners to improve teaching and learning. Benefits of computers in education Computers can:
Enhance engagement Provide personalized learning experiences Give access to a wide range of resources Help students develop essential technology skills Challenges of computers in education, One of the main challenges is the digital divide, which means that not all students have equal access to technology and reliable internet connections.
Use of computers in schools,
The number of schools with computers has increased, but computers are still mainly used to teach students about computers, and not integrated into existing subjects.
